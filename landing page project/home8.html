INDIAN CRICKET TEAM

The Indian cricket team, governed by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI), is one of the most prominent and successful teams in international cricket. Known for their passionate fan base and rich cricketing history, the team has produced legendary cricketers like Sachin Tendulkar, Kapil Dev, and Virat Kohli. India won its first Cricket World Cup in 1983 under Kapil Dev's captaincy and clinched the title again in 2011, led by Mahendra Singh Dhoni. The team is also known for its strong performances in other formats, including the T20 World Cup victory in 2007 and numerous triumphs in Test cricket. With a blend of experienced players and emerging talents, the Indian cricket team continues to be a formidable force on the global stage.
